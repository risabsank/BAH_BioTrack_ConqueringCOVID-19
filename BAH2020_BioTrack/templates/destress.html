Learn How to Destress from the Comfort of Your Home

Often times, people are often found with hours of work and nearing deadlines, and they struggle to find a way to destress and relax. In order to help, we found effective techniques that people can use for many situations and from the comfort of their homes.

Deep Breathing Techniques

There are many effective ways to relax your mind by breathing in different patterns. In this video, physical trainer Michelle Kenway describes basic deep breathing techniques that can be used at any time during the day.
Meditation

Meditation is an easy way to reduce stress by relaxing the mind. It is a great method to calm down and clear your mind. In this video, yoga instructor Rosalie walks you through a meditation session.
Relaxing Music

Listening to relaxing music can help keep your mind stress free and rested. Here, you can listen to the relaxing sounds of nature for up to 3 hours as you relieve yourself of all stress and pressure.
Exercise

Exercising is beneficial to your mental state and this can be done from the comfort of your home. In this video, physical trainer Tom Holland walks you through easy exercises that you can do daily.
Journaling

Journaling is an effective way to sort out your thoughts and prepare yourself mentally for the day. In this video, a YouTuber teaches you how to organize your thoughts and ideas to become more goal-oriented.
